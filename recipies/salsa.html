Green Salsa Recipie

Overview

In this article, we will go over the recipie to preapre a delicious spicy green salsa (goes very well with both taco recipies!) This recipie is very simple and beginner friendly.

Ingredients

  • 5 Tomatillos
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 4-5 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 Small Avocados
  • 3 Jalapeño Peppers
  • 3 Serrano Peppers
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Pepper
  • 1/4 cup of Water

Preparation Time

  • Approximately 15 minutes

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water, heat, and bring to a boil
  2. Add the tomatillos, jalapeños, and serrano peppers
  3. Boil until tomatillos change to a slightly yellow color
  4. Add all the ingredients to a blender until a smooth/creamy texture is achieved
  5. On a medium container pass the contents of the blender through a strainer to remove the perpper seeds
  6. (optional) add more salt if desired
