Egg & Ham Breakfast Tacos Recipe

Overview

In this article, we will go over the recipe to prepare some delicious egg & ham breakfast tacos! This recipe is simple and beginner friendly. This recipe will be enough for three servings of two tacos each.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of ham (chop into small cubes)
  • 8 eggs
  • Corn/flour torillas
  • 1/ tbsp Pepper

Preparation Time

  • Approximately 15 minutes

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium heat and add oil (spray or liquid)
  2. Add ham and cook until gold/light brown color (~4-5 mins)
  3. Add eggs and scramble them in the pan
  4. Once the eggs are fluffy, add the pepper and stir
  5. Heat toritllas and add the ham & egg
  6. (optional) add salsa if desired

(optional) this recipe will go well with green salsa for those who would like a little spice to their food. You can find a recipe to a green salsa in this page
