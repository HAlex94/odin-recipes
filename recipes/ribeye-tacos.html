Ribeye Tacos Recipe

Overview

In this article, we will go over the recipe to prepare some delicious ribeye steak tacos! This recipe is simple and beginner friendly. It will be enough for three servings of two tacos each.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak (~1 lb)
  • Corn torillas
  • 1/2 Onion
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Sliced Raddish
  • Avocado
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Steak Seasoning (your choice)

Preparation Time

  • Approximately 20 minutes

Steps

  1. Season ribeye steak with 1/2 tbsp of salt, pepper, garlic powder, and steak seasoning on each side.
  2. Heat pan to medium-high heat and add 1 tbsp of butter.
  3. Once butter is melting add steak and begin cooking it.
  4. Cook each side for ~8 minutes for a medium rare steak (can cook for longer depending on your preference).
  5. Chop the steak into even sized chunks (recommend to cut vertically for a better texture).
  6. (optional) add more salt if desired
  7. Heat torillas
  8. Add steak
  9. Add onion
  10. Add cilantro
  11. Add raddish
  12. Add slices of avocado
  13. Add lime

(optional) this recipe will go well with green salsa for those who would like a little spice to their food. You can find a recipe to a green salsa in this page
